Our Journey Towards aSustainable Food System : Highlights from Past Events

Awareness Seminars

We’ve taken our message directly to 400 schools and colleges with engaging speaker sessions reaching over 90,000 people. Equipped with our educational material introducing various aspects of a sustainable food system and health, our speakers have inspired audiences to take the 21-day dairy-free challenge. These sessions are not just informative but interactive, sparking conversations and encouraging pledges for a healthier future.

Online Webinars

Our virtual platform has connected us with nutritionists and students alike. Through hour-long webinars, we’ve conducted sessions delving deep into topics surrounding a sustainable food system and nutrition. From insightful presentations to lively Q&A sessions, our webinars have been a hit among students, accessible anytime, anywhere.

Documentary Screenings

We’ve brought eye-opening documentaries to communities far and wide. Facilitating over 25 documentary screenings covering everything from the impact of animal agriculture to the benefits of a sustainable food system and plant-based diets, our screenings have sparked conversations and inspired change. By including documentaries in local languages, we’ve ensured that our message reaches diverse audiences, making a real difference in communities around the country.

‘Share a Meal’ at Our Potlucks

Food brings people together, and our potlucks have been no exception. Sharing delicious plant-based meals has been a joyous occasion, fostering a sense of community and camaraderie among participants while highlighting the importance of a sustainable food system. We have conducted 80 potlucks, engaging a community of 2000 people.

Plant Powered Day Initiative

In July 2024, we launched our Plant Powered Day initiative, wherein plant-based meals are served 1 day on campus in schools and colleges. Till date, we have partnered with 18 schools across 6 Indian cities and served 100,000 plant-based meals.
