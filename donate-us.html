Inspiring Change Through Plant-Based Advocacy

At Food and You 2.0, we believe in the transformative power of food. Through education, awareness, and hands-on engagement, we are pioneering a shift towards plant-based lifestyles in India. Our efforts are not just about food choices—they are about building a compassionate and sustainable future for all.

Step 1:-
Our Mission
At Food and You 2.0, we believe that every donation creates a ripple effect—bringing us closer to a healthier, sustainable, and compassionate world. Through education, awareness, and hands-on initiatives, we empower individuals and communities to embrace plant-based choices for a better future.
Step 2:-
Collaborating with Schools
We partner with schools to introduce Plant-Based Meal Days, making sustainable and cruelty-free food choices accessible to students. By working with school administrations, we help incorporate plant-based options into daily meals, fostering a new generation of mindful eaters.
Step 3:-
Educating for Change
Through interactive sessions, workshops, and awareness programs, we teach students about the nutritional, environmental, and ethical benefits of a plant-based diet. Our goal is to inspire lifelong, conscious eating habits that contribute to better health and a kinder world.
Step 4:-
Empowering Vol & Advocates
We train and equip volunteers with the right knowledge and tools to promote compassionate food choices in their communities. By fostering a strong network of advocates, we ensure that the message of sustainability and ethical eating spreads far and wide, creating a lasting impact.
Step 5:-
Your Impact
Every contribution directly supports our efforts in reaching more schools, conducting impactful educational programs, and training passionate volunteers. With just ₹500, you can help introduce a Plant-Based Meal Day in a school, benefiting hundreds of students.

Our Impact Across States

West Bengal: Plant-Based Awareness
  • Food Festivals in Kolkata – We set up plant-based stalls at key public events, reaching 1,000+ students and visitors with tastings and awareness campaigns.
  • School & College Outreach – More than 28,000 students pledged to go dairy-free after attending workshops on the impact of dairy and animal agriculture.
Assam: Sustainable Eating in Schools
  • Plant-Powered Thursdays – In collaboration with Pathshala High School, we are serving over 7,000 plant-based meals per year, inspiring students to embrace sustainable eating.
  • "Weight Off Your Plate" Initiative – This project educated 1,000+ students on the carbon footprint of animal agriculture and the benefits of plant-based protein.
Manipur: Building a Community for Change
  • Plant-Based Potlucks & Film Screenings – Community events are sparking conversations about sustainable diets and encouraging families to adopt plant-based meals.
  • Engagement with Local Leaders – We are working with Manipur-based activists and educators to integrate plant-forward messaging into local sustainability programs.
Delhi
  • A landmark event at the Supreme Court of India introduced plant-based cuisine to 330 participants, including advocates and staff.
  • This tasting session marked a significant milestone, bringing the conversation about plant-based diets to the nation's highest judicial venue.
  • Encouraged more individuals to embrace a plant-based lifestyle.
Maharashtra
  • Supreme Court Tasting Session – 330+ legal professionals participated in a plant-based food tasting event, marking a historic moment in mainstreaming plant-based diets.
  • 9 Expert-Led Ask Me Anything Sessions – Empowering participants with credible insights on plant-based nutrition and lifestyle.

Your donation supports our efforts to partner with schools, implement plant-based meal days, and educate students about the benefits of compassionate and sustainable diets. This ensures that students receive balanced nutrition, develop empathy for animals, and adopt practices that reduce animal suffering.

Plant-based diets significantly reduce the demand for animal products, leading to fewer animals being raised and slaughtered. By promoting plant-based meals, we help combat factory farming practices that cause immense animal suffering.

Every plant-based meal day in schools translates to fewer animals subjected to the harsh realities of factory farming. By choosing compassionate food options, we are actively reducing the demand that drives animal suffering. Your support helps create a kinder world where animals are respected and protected.
